The Dots

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Muuto, Denmark

Coat hooks of solid oak or painted ash wood. The hooks come in several sizes. Available in sets of five or as individual hooks. The original Dots are made in wood. Metal Dots and Ceramic Dots have later been added to the Dots family.
